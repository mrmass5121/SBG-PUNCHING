Our Services

Comprehensive sheet metal manufacturing under one roof — from raw material to finished component.

01
CNC PUNCHING

High-speed CNC turret punching for MS, SS, aluminium and GI sheets. Consistent precision on batch and bulk orders with zero tolerances.

02
LASER CUTTING

Fiber laser cutting up to 20mm thickness across MS, SS and aluminium. Burr-free edges, complex contours, and minimal heat distortion.

SHEET BENDING

CNC press brake bending up to 10mm. Profiles, channels, boxes — any angle, any material. Repeatability ensured across large production runs.
